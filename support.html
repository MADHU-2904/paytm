Top Questions

How do I link my bank account? expand_more

To link your bank account, go to the 'Profile' section, select 'Payment Settings', and click on 'Saved Payment Details'. Choose 'Add New Bank Account', select your bank from the list, and verify via SMS.

Why is my transaction pending? expand_more

Transactions may be pending due to network issues with the bank. Usually, these are resolved within 24-48 hours. If the amount was deducted but the transaction failed, it will be automatically refunded.

How to complete KYC? expand_more

You can complete your KYC by visiting a nearby KYC point or through Video KYC on the app. Ensure you have your PAN card and Aadhaar card ready for the process.

How to reset my password? expand_more

Go to the login screen and click on 'Forgot Password'. Enter your registered mobile number to receive an OTP. Verify the OTP and set a new password.
